What is Investiture?

In Brandon Sanderson's Cosmere universe, Investiture is the fundamental magical energy that permeates all worlds. Each planet manifests this power differently, creating distinct magic systems with their own rules and limitations. This scaffold borrows the name because, like Investiture itself, it's a foundation that can manifest as many different things.

Scadrial

Allomancy

Users ingest and "burn" metals to gain specific powers. Each of the sixteen Allomantic metals grants a different ability, from enhanced strength to emotional manipulation to seeing the future.

Scadrial

Feruchemy

A magic of balance. Feruchemists store attributes (strength, speed, memory, warmth) in metal "metalminds" for later use. What you store, you can withdraw—nothing is created or destroyed.

Scadrial

Hemalurgy

The dark art of stealing abilities through metal spikes driven through living beings. It transfers powers between people but always at a cost—something is lost in the transfer.

Roshar

Surgebinding

Knights Radiant bond with spren (cognitive entities) to access Surges—fundamental forces like gravity, friction, and transformation. Each order commands two of the ten Surges.

Roshar

Voidbinding

The corrupted counterpart to Surgebinding, associated with Odium. Less understood, it grants powers to the singers and Fused through different means than the Radiant bonds.

Sel

AonDor

The magic of the Elantrians. Practitioners draw Aons—symbolic characters—in the air to produce magical effects. The power is tied to the land itself and the city of Elantris.

Nalthis

Awakening

Uses BioChromatic Breath to animate objects with Commands. Everyone is born with one Breath; accumulating more grants heightened senses and abilities. Color is consumed in the process.

Taldain

Sand Mastery

Controls the white sand of the Dayside, which holds Investiture from the sun. Sand masters can command the sand to move and shape itself, though it drains their hydration.

Threnody

Threnodite Magic

A world haunted by shades—cognitive shadows of the dead. The magic here involves silver's protective properties and the dangerous Simple Rules that keep the living safe from spirits.

First of the Sun

Aviar Bonds

Birds called Aviar develop magical talents by eating fruit from trees on the islands. When bonded to humans, they share abilities like hiding one's mind from predators.

The Cosmere is the shared universe of Brandon Sanderson's adult fantasy novels. Learn more at brandonsanderson.com
